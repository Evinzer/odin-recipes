Pancakes

This recipe will make 6 delicious pancakes

  1. Crack 3 eggs and whisk together
  2. Add flour and milk untill you get a nice smooth consistency
  3. Add a pinch of salt, and 2 spoons of sugar
  4. Add some melted butter
  5. Turn pan to medium/high heat

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 gallon of milk
  • Flour,salt and sugar
  • Some toppings, bacon,blueberries or maple syrup
